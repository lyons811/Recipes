Orange Chicken with rice

This is a fairly simple recipe. All you do is buy some store brand orange chicken, and follow the directions on the package. You then start the rice in a rice cooker, I recommend jasmine rice.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Orange Chicken
  • Saracha
  • Soy Sause
  • Spciey peanut sauce

Steps

  1. Follow instructions on Orange Chicken package
  2. Make rice in rice maker
  3. Wait appox 30-40 mins for the Orange Chicken and rice to be done
  4. Put Sarcha, Soy Sause and Spice peanut sauce on all
  5. Enjoy!
